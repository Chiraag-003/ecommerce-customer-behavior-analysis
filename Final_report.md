 📄 Customer Behavior & Revenue Analysis Report



📌 Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze customer behavior and revenue patterns in an e-commerce dataset to generate actionable business insights that support better decision-making.

🧾 Dataset Overview

The dataset contains transaction-level data including:

* Order Date
* Product Name
* Category
* Region
* Quantity
* Sales
* Profit

The data represents sales transactions across different regions and product categories.

 🔍 Analysis Performed

 1. Repeat Purchase Behavior

* Identified product-region combinations with multiple orders
* Highlighted patterns indicating customer preference and repeat behavior

 2. Purchase Frequency Analysis

* Calculated total quantity sold per product
* Identified high-demand products based on purchase volume

3. Revenue Analysis

* Determined top revenue-generating products
* Analyzed revenue contribution by category
* Evaluated regional sales performance

 📈 Key Insights

* High-demand products do not always generate the highest revenue, highlighting the difference between volume and value
* A small group of products contributes significantly to total revenue
* Certain product-region combinations show strong repeat purchase patterns
* Revenue distribution varies across categories and regions
* The West region shows strong overall sales performance

💡 Business Recommendations

* Focus on high-revenue products to maximize profitability
* Optimize pricing for high-demand but lower-revenue items
* Expand operations in high-performing regions
* Improve inventory planning based on demand patterns
* Leverage repeat purchase trends for targeted marketing strategies

📊 Business Impact

This analysis can help businesses:

* Improve product strategy
* Optimize pricing decisions
* Focus on high-performing regions
* Increase revenue through data-driven insights

🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Python (Pandas, Matplotlib)
* Power BI
* Jupyter Notebook

🚀 Conclusion

This project demonstrates how raw e-commerce data can be transformed into meaningful insights that help businesses understand customer behavior and improve revenue strategies through data-driven decision-making.
